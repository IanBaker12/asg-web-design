FAQ

Questions we hear a lot.

How much does a website cost?

It depends on the scope, but most small-business sites fall between $1,500 and $6,000. We always quote a flat fee in writing before starting — no hourly surprises.

How long does it take?

A simple brochure site typically launches in 2–3 weeks. Larger e-commerce or custom builds usually take 4–8 weeks. The biggest variable is how fast you can review and approve along the way.

Do I own the site when we're done?

Yes — 100%. You own the domain, the code, the content, everything. We don't lock you into proprietary platforms you can't leave.

Can you redesign my existing site?

Absolutely. We do redesigns regularly. We'll preserve your SEO, content, and what's working — and rebuild what isn't.

What if I need changes after launch?

Two options: pay for changes as they come up, or join a monthly care plan that includes a set number of updates plus security, backups, and hosting.
